Globe Rules

A random country is chosen — can you guess which one it is?

Type a country name and press Guess. After each guess, the country will be colored on the globe based on how close it is to the target.

You also get the distance in km between your guess and the target country.

If your guess shares a border with the target, you'll get a special notification.

Press Give Up to reveal the answer.
