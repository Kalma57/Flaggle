Flaggle Rules

Can you identify the country by its flag?

Enter a country name and press GUESS. After each attempt, you'll see visual feedback comparing your guess to the correct flag. Use the hints to get closer to the answer.

Guess correctly to win — or press GIVE UP to reveal the solution.

Challenge yourself to solve it in the fewest attempts possible!
